Path of Magical Words

word building game


Goals:

Explore the entire playing field for 100 points.

Use as many objects as you can, for 10 points each and an extra round, plus a 100-point reward at the end of the game if you can do it all.

Use as many pre-written letters as you can, for 5 points each and an extra turn per 5 letters, plus a 50-point reward at the end of the game if you get them all right.

Collect as many of the 1-9 point rewards as possible.

Make as many words as you can for rewards based on the number of new letters you create in a round:

  • 5 letters - 5 points
  • 6 letters - 10 points
  • 7 letters - 15 points
  • 8 letters - 20 points
  • 9 letters - 25 points
